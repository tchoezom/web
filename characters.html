Important Characters

The protagonist of the Webtoon is Anthanasia de Alger Obelia. She is 15 years old and is the crown princess of the royal family. She has developed, to some extent, the ability to wield magic.















Another important character is Claude de Alger Obelia. He is Anthasia's biological father and the current reigning emperor of the Obelian Empire. He is wise, extremely capable of magic, and also very stern.

































Jennette Margarita is a supporting character of the story. She is kind, in want of care, and is friends with Anthasia. She believes she is a member of the royal family, alhtough her true bloodline isn't confirmed.

























Lucas is a magician and Anthasia's friend. He takes care of Anthasia and although he comes off as intimidating due to his magical strength, he is the official companion of the crown princess.
