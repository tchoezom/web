Characters


The protagonist of the Webtoon is Athanasia de Alger Obelia. She is 15 years old and is the crown princess of the royal family. She has developed, to some extent, the ability to wield magic.
Another important character is Claude de Alger Obelia. He is Anthasia's biological father and the current reigning emperor of the Obelian Empire. He is wise, extremely capable of magic, and also very stern.
Jennette Margarita is a supporting character of the story. She is kind, in want of care, and is friends with Anthasia. She believes she is a member of the royal family, alhtough her true bloodline isn't confirmed.
Lucas is a magician and Anthasia's friend. He takes care of Anthasia and although he comes off as intimidating due to his magical strength, he is the official companion of the crown princess.

Plot


This webcomic may seem confusing at first, but to a regular manhwa reader, the plot is not really anything that special. Suddenly Became a Princess One Day, or otherwise known as Who Made Me a Princess is about a girl who reincarnates, or finds herself in a fictional story, The Lovely Princess , as the abandoned princess, Anthasia, of the Obelian Empire. In the story she goes into, Athanasia was mercilessly executed by her ruthless father, Emperor Claude de Alger Obelia, for supposedly poisoning the princess, Jennette Margarita. To change the outcome of the story and avoid death Athanasia tries her best to build an good relationship between herself and her new father, Claude, to survive.
