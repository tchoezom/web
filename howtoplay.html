HOW TO PLAY

The rules to "Rock, Paper, Scissors" are pretty simple! The game will require two players.

Both players will repeat, "rock, paper, scissors, shoot", and on "shoot", both players will choose rock, paper, or scissors. "Rock" is shown by a fist. "Paper" is shown by an open hand. "Scissors" is shown by a peace sign.

After both players put forward their hand, it is time to determine who the winner is. If both players chose the same sign, then it is a draw. Otherwise, paper beats rock by covering it, rock beats scissors by smashing it, and scissors beat paper by cutting it. That's it! Enjoy playing!
