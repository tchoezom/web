Recently, the popularity of "manhwas", or webcomics as they are also called, have sky-rocketed. Originating from South Korea, websites that publish digital webcomics, also known as "webtoons" were launched globally. Since then, more and more people are becoming fans of these webtoons. There are endless varieties and genres, ranging from romance, mystery, fantasy, etc. One fan-favorite is a webtoon called Suddenly Became a Princess One Day. It combines magic, fantasy, and royalty to take us through Anthasia's story.
